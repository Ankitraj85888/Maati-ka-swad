Our Story

How one woman's recipe book became a national brand.

The Beginning

From a Recipe Notebook to 50,000 Homes

In 2019, Sunita Devi, a homemaker in Jaipur, decided to share what her mother-in-law had passed down to her — a tattered notebook filled with handwritten recipes for pickles, spice blends, sweets, and snacks.

She started small. A few jars at a time. Neighbors would show up at her door asking for more. Then their neighbors. Then people from the next street. Within a year, WhatsApp groups were buzzing with "where can I order Sunita ji's achaar?"

Today, Maati Ka Swad — "Taste of the Soil" — ships to over 350 cities across India. But nothing has changed in the kitchen: same recipes, same care, same love.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌿

Purity

No preservatives, no artificial colors, no shortcuts. What you read on the label is exactly what goes in your food — nothing more, nothing less.

📜

Tradition

We don't "modernize" our recipes. We protect them. Every product reflects generations of Indian culinary wisdom — unchanged and uncompromised.

❤️

Love

Food made with love tastes different. We genuinely care about what we make and who we make it for. Every jar is packed like it's going to our own family.

🤝

Fair Trade

We source directly from farmers at fair prices. When you buy from us, you support not just our family, but the farming communities we work with.

How We Make It

Our Craft Process

No industrial machines. No assembly lines. Just careful hands and time-tested methods.

🌾

1. Source

Ingredients hand-picked from trusted local farmers. Fresh, seasonal, and responsibly sourced.

☀️

2. Prepare

Sun-drying, stone-grinding, and slow-roasting processes preserve the full natural flavor.
